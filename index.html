Earth Digital Twin

Own a country on the Solana blockchain. Buy it, name your price, hold it, flip it.

Built on Solana Only 195 NFTs — one per country 100% price cap per sale

This is a planet, tokenised. Every country on Earth — exactly 195 of them — exists as a unique NFT on the Solana network. There will never be more. You can own Brazil, Iran, the United Kingdom, or any country you have a connection to.

The rules are simple. Each country starts at a price based on its natural resources. Buy a country and you own its NFT. The price automatically jumps 20% for the next buyer — but you can set your own price up to a 100% increase (a hard ceiling, so no one can sit on a country forever). Sell whenever someone meets your price. Repeat.

Why this matters. 10% of every sale goes back to the protocol to keep the lights on. The other 90% goes to you, the seller. Your country is yours until someone wants it more. The leaderboard tracks the 10 most expensive countries in real time — own one and watch the world fight to take it from you.
